How do I create my first flyer? ‚ñº

Creating your first flyer is simple:

  1. Open the CRE Flyer Maker app
  2. Tap the "+" button to start a new flyer
  3. Take a photo of the property or select from your gallery
  4. Fill in the property details (title, price, size, type, etc.)
  5. Add broker information and contact details
  6. Preview and customize your flyer
  7. Share or export your professional flyer!
What property types are supported? ‚ñº

CRE Flyer Maker supports all major commercial property types:

  • Office - Office buildings, co-working spaces, medical offices
  • Retail - Shopping centers, standalone retail, restaurants
  • Industrial - Warehouses, manufacturing facilities, distribution centers
  • Land - Development sites, vacant land, investment opportunities

Yes! CRE Flyer Maker offers several customization options:

  • Customize text formatting and positioning

QR codes in CRE Flyer Maker provide smart sharing capabilities:

  • Easy for prospects to scan and save contact info

Share your flyers through multiple channels:

How do I upgrade to Pro? ‚ñº

Upgrading to Pro is easy:

  1. Open the CRE Flyer Maker app
  2. Go to Settings or tap "Upgrade to Pro" when prompted
  3. Choose your subscription plan (monthly or annual)
  4. Complete the purchase through the App Store
  5. Enjoy unlimited flyers and Pro features!

Pro subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID and can be cancelled anytime in your App Store settings.

Troubleshooting and technical assistance

Common Issues & Solutions

App crashes or won't open ‚ñº

If the app is crashing or won't open, try these steps:

  1. Force close the app and reopen it
  2. Restart your iPhone/iPad
  3. Check if you have the latest version of the app
  4. Update to the latest iOS version
  5. If the issue persists, delete and reinstall the app

Note: Reinstalling will remove saved flyers, so make sure to export them first!

Photos not loading or appearing blurry ‚ñº

For photo issues, try the following:

  • Check camera permissions in Settings > Privacy & Security > Camera
  • Ensure you have sufficient storage space on your device
  • Clean your camera lens
  • Try taking photos in good lighting conditions
  • For existing photos, check if they're stored in iCloud and need to download
Sharing or export not working ‚ñº

If you can't share or export flyers:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Verify app permissions for the sharing method you're using
  • Try a different sharing option (email, save to photos, etc.)
  • Restart the app and try again
  • Check available storage space on your device
Subscription or billing issues ‚ñº

For subscription-related issues:

  • Check your subscription status in Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions
  • Ensure your payment method is up to date
  • Try restoring purchases in the app settings
  • Contact Apple Support for billing disputes
  • Email us at support@creflyer.app for account-specific issues
