1. Overview

CRE Flyer Maker ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

üîí Our Privacy Commitment

We believe in transparency and your right to privacy. We collect only the information necessary to provide you with the best possible experience using CRE Flyer Maker.

By using CRE Flyer Maker, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When you use CRE Flyer Maker, you may provide us with certain information, including:

  • Property Information: Property details, descriptions, addresses, prices, and specifications you enter
  • Broker Information: Your name, company, phone number, and email address
  • Photos: Property images you capture or select from your device
  • Account Information: Information for app preferences and settings

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect certain information when you use our app:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Features used, time spent in app, crash reports
  • Technical Data: IP address, app version, device settings

üì± Local Storage

Most of your data (property information, photos, flyers) is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly choose to share content.

2.3 Camera and Photo Access

CRE Flyer Maker requires access to your device's camera and photo library to:

  • Capture new property photos
  • Select existing photos from your gallery
  • Create and save flyer images

You can control these permissions through your device settings at any time.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

3.1 Core App Functionality

  • Create and generate professional property flyers
  • Store your property information and preferences
  • Enable photo capture and editing features
  • Generate QR codes for property sharing

3.2 App Improvement

  • Analyze app usage to improve features and performance
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Develop new features based on user needs
  • Ensure app security and prevent fraud

3.3 Customer Support

  • Respond to your questions and support requests
  • Provide technical assistance
  • Send important app updates and notifications

üö´ What We DON'T Do

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. We do not use your property photos or information for any purpose other than providing our services to you.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

4.1 With Your Consent

When you explicitly choose to share flyers or property information through the app's sharing features (email, social media, messaging apps), we facilitate that sharing according to your instructions.

4.2 Service Providers

We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our app:

  • Analytics Services: To understand app usage and improve performance
  • Cloud Storage: For backup and synchronization features (if enabled)
  • Customer Support: To provide technical assistance

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request, or to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Ensure user safety
  • Investigate fraud or security issues

4.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change in ownership or control.

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Local Storage

Most of your data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Property information and details
  • Photos and flyer designs
  • App preferences and settings
  • Broker contact information

üîê Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5.2 Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Local Data: Stored on your device until you delete the app or clear the data
  • Usage Analytics: Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained for up to 2 years

5.3 Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication and access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal information by employees

6. Your Rights

You have several rights regarding your personal information:

6.1 Access and Control

  • Access: You can view all your property information and settings within the app
  • Edit: You can modify or update your information at any time
  • Delete: You can delete individual properties or all data from the app
  • Export: You can export your flyers and property information

6.2 Privacy Controls

  • Camera Access: Control camera and photo library permissions in device settings
  • Sharing: Choose what information to include when sharing flyers
  • Analytics: Opt out of usage analytics in app settings

6.3 Data Portability

You can export your property information and flyers in common formats (PDF, image files) for use with other applications or services.

7. Children's Privacy

CRE Flyer Maker is designed for professional use by real estate professionals and is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information as quickly as possible.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@creflyer.app.

  • We comply with applicable data protection laws in your jurisdiction
